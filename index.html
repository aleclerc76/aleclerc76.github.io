Hi nice visitor.
I am a PhD student in the LMI in Rouen, under the supervision of Nicolas Forcadel and Hasnaa Zidani. The aim of my thesis is to study Hamilton-Jacobi equations in some metric geodesic spaces with curvature properties. This topic gathers ODEs outside of vector spaces, control problems, viscosity solutions and uses the tools of differentiation in curved spaces (Alexandrov, Wasserstein...). By the way, I will be looking for a post-doc, starting in September 2025.
